Basic Computer Course:

  • Start with a catchy introduction: "Welcome to the world of computers! In this Basic Computer Course, you will learn how to navigate the digital landscape and develop essential computer skills."
  • The main goal of this course is to provide you with a solid foundation in computer literacy. By the end of this course, you will be able to confidently operate a computer, understand basic software applications, and effectively use the internet.
  • Throughout the course, you will explore various topics, including computer hardware and software, operating systems, file management, word processing, spreadsheet creation, and internet browsing.
  • Our teaching approach focuses on hands-on learning, where you will have ample opportunities to practice what you learn. You will work with real-world examples and exercises to reinforce your understanding.
  • The Basic Computer Course typically spans over a period of 12 months. Classes are conducted 5 times a week for 4 hours each session.
  • By the end of this course, you will have the confidence to navigate and utilize various computer applications in both personal and professional settings. You'll also be well-prepared for more advanced computer courses and future technological advancements.
