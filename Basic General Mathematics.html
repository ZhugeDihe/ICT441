Basic General Mathematics Course:

  • The Basic General Mathematics course is designed to provide students with a fundamental understanding of mathematical concepts that are commonly used in everyday life. This course is suitable for individuals who may not have a strong background in mathematics but still need to develop basic math skills for various purposes.
  • The course covers various topics such as arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division), fractions, decimals, percentages, basic algebra, and geometry. These topics are taught in a step-by-step manner, starting with the basics and gradually building upon them.
  • Throughout the course, students will learn practical applications of mathematics. They will develop skills that can be used in real-life situations such as budgeting, calculating discounts, interpreting data, and solving everyday problems. The course also emphasizes problem-solving techniques, logical reasoning, and critical thinking skills.
  • The Basic General Mathematics course is interactive and engaging, with a combination of theoretical lessons, practical examples, and exercises to reinforce learning. Students will have the opportunity to practice their skills and receive feedback from instructors to ensure their understanding of the material.
  • By the end of the course, students should have a solid foundation in basic mathematical concepts and be able to apply them effectively in their daily lives. This course serves as a stepping stone for further studies in more advanced mathematics subjects or as a means to enhance one's mathematical literacy.
